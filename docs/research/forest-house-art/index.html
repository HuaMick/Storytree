Canopy Treehouse 8 geometry fixes applied

A cozy plank hut hugs a towering story-tree that grows straight up through its platform and out the roof into a glowing canopy — rope ladder, branch-walkway, and lanterns swaying in the leaves, a home you climb into among the treetops.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor right deck edge, root/branch swoosh from trunk — decorative swoosh drapes over the deck edge in front of the new railing, making its depth order ambiguous
  • minor trunk pass-through at deck level — the deck cut-out reads as a hard-edged dark parallelogram slightly wider than the trunk

Coastal Lighthouse 7 geometry fixes applied

A cream-and-teal striped tower tapers up to a blazing glazed lantern room whose rotating beacon fans golden rays out to sea and pools as a warm reflection in the tide, while a whitewashed keeper's cottage with a glowing window and a smoking chimney nestles at its foot on a rocky, grass-tufted headland edge.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor left of cottage, at the roof's lower-left eave tip — relocated rock is drawn over the overhanging eave tip — occlusion inverted (roof should be in front); a few pixels, reads as 'tucked' at full size
  • minor tide pool, front-right of tile — pool rim is still tangent-tight to the tile's front edge bevel; and the two white foam flecks pinned at the rim's exact left/right vertices read as rivets

Coastal Stilt House 15 geometry fixes applied

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor right-side ramp/gangway from deck to ground — ramp foot and lower railing segment meet the ground slightly ambiguously (railing reads a touch detached)

Crystal Lumen House 6 geometry fixes applied

A translucent faceted gem-dwelling that drinks in the map's own wisp-glow and radiates it back — cool teal-and-indigo crystal walls lit from within by a molten golden heart, crowned by a spire of quartz roof-facets that throws refracted light rays into the dark and cradles a single wisp at its glinting apex.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor flanking spires, just below each spire's base at the eave line — small detached crystal shard floats disconnected from the spire body (carryover from current; ambiguous whether intentional motif)
  • minor left facade diamond window — window straddles the wall panel-seam junction, with grid seams passing visibly behind/through it (carryover from current)

Forest Windmill 7 geometry fixes applied

A tapering stone windmill caught mid-turn — four cream-sailed lattice arms sweep past a teal-domed cap while golden light spills from arched windows and a timber balcony rings the tower, a working landmark you could hear creaking in the breeze.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor mid-tower balcony band, center-front and right end — band terminates in downward-pointing dart spikes that read as torn fabric rather than structure
  • minor lower-left tower wall — new small rectangular window breaks the arched-window language of the other openings
  • minor left sail blade at balcony height — railing/band renders over the sail blade though sails should sweep outside the gallery (layering ambiguity, present in both versions)

Hamlet Cluster 6 geometry fixes applied

Four varied micro-houses — a teal gable, a wheat pyramid, a little green cottage and a tall indigo chimney-house — huddle around a lantern-lit stone well on one shared grass tile, stitched together by glowing stepping-stone paths and a drift of fireflies, so a dense story reads at a glance as a whole living village rather than a single lonely roof.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor purple tall house (right), roof/attic — Steep dark roof and attic-story wall merge into one near-uniform navy mass; face separation is too weak to read the roof planes cleanly
  • minor small green house (lower-left), pyramid roof — Both visible roof faces are nearly the same dark value, so the pyramid can momentarily read concave/hollow at full-scene scale

Hillside Burrow 6 geometry fixes applied

A cozy hobbit-home dug straight into a wildflower-freckled grassy dome — its round green plank door glows brass-knobbed at the heart of an earthen cut-away, portholes peek from the turf, and a coursed-stone chimney trails hearth-smoke over exposed root-laced strata that make the whole mound feel lifted from the map's under-island roots.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor doorway recess, either side of the arch — brown dug-out face meets the grass in hard straight vertical seams, reading as a rectangular cut-out panel rather than a dug entry
  • minor right dome silhouette, mid-height — dark shaded face terminates in a sharp leaf-tip cusp against the outline

Storybook Cottage 3 geometry fixes applied

Tiered Pagoda 3 geometry fixes applied

Trail Gatehouse 8 geometry fixes applied

A stone gate-tower straddles the map's trail on twin battered piers, its keystoned arch opening a lantern-lit tunnel that reveals a sunlit clearing beyond, while a jettied timber house with a golden oriel bay window and a smoking slate roof crowns the passage — the swinging iron lantern over the arch pulling fireflies out of the dark.

Known defects remaining (minor, all)

  • minor stepping-stone cluster, mid-path just above the island corner (~x420-560, y940-1010) — stones overlap after being compacted; one pair has the farther (upper) stone drawn over the nearer (lower) stone — inverted occlusion order
  • minor mushrooms, left plinth edge (~x280-340, y890-950) — mushrooms straddle the grass/dirt fold and effectively grow out of the sloped dirt face (present in both versions)
  • minor new right support column under the jetty beam (~x790-830, y560-880) — column face has no stone coursing while the pier beside it does, and its top is lighter than the pier front — slight material/shading inconsistency
